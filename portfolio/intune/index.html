intune

  • overview

    InTune is a mobile app that lets users stay in touch with their loved ones using just voice notes. In today's fast paced world, every stimulus aims to grab your attention using mainly visuals. Even if there are auditory components associated with visuals, they are not the sole focus. I wanted to create an experience where people's voices are captured and cherished, at a time where everything seems fleeting and momentary.

    client: Graduate Course
    when: Feb-May 2022
    role: Visual & Interaction Designer
    process: Ideation, Conception, Sketching, Wireframing, Visual Exploration, High-Fidelity Prototype
    tools: Figma
