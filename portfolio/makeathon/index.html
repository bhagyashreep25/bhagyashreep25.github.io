visa design challenge - makeathon project

  • overview

    To challenge myself and test my critical thinking skills, I took part in a UX design makeathon, along with a friend. We decided to take on the challenge sponsored by Visa, aimed at helping minority-owned small business owners setup digital payments. We carried out research, realized the solution and built a high-fidelity prototype all within 24 hours. To top this amazing learning experience, we ended up winning second place for our efforts!

    client: Visa
    when: Apr 2022
    role: UX Researcher & Designer on a team of two
    process: Literature Review, Conception, Sketching, Wireframing, High-Fidelity Prototype
    tools: Figma


  • ux research

    We performed literature review to better understand our target users, small businesses, digital payments and how the three coalesce together.

    After performing literature review, I used the design thinking approach to come up with a set of "How Might We" questions and use them to ideate our solutions.
