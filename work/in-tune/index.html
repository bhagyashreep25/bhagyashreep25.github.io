CONCEPT
Key Functionalities


VOICE-BASED COMMUNICATION

Send & reply to voice notes

Add images/videos to reinforce voice notes

Archive voice notes to create a playlist of a contact
TRENDS

Review own weekly voice notes highlights

Review friends' weekly voice note highlights
TUNEBOT: VOICE-BASED COMMANDS

Fetch & retrieve specific notes

Ask basic questions to explore trends in detail

Allows for touch-less interaction
AUTOMATED MOOD SENSING OF VOICE NOTES

Analyzes sent & received voice notes to deduce mood of the speaker

Uses color to convey this mood when notes are played

PROCESS
Iterating on the wireframes

I conducted a round of testing with my classmates to check for intuitiveness and also received feedback from the professor. I made some key decisions going forward:

1. Implementing a conventional chat-like model instead of time-based view for ease of use.
2. Moving the record audio (+) button inside each contact's chat view
3. Adding screens for trends and tunebot



PROCESS
Game changer: Light mode to Dark mode

By changing the theme from light mode to dark mode, I improved the accessibility of the screens as well as allowed the important elements to attract attention.
